Information about how to work with this application

What is this program for?

This program solves the direct and inverse problems of kinematics.

Direct problem of kinematics

You need to choose the lengths of your links, the angle of rotation of the first link and the angle of rotation of the second link. Then click on the button.

Inverse problem of kinematics

You need to choose the lengths of your links, to select the X and the Y coordinates where the manipulator will come. Then click on the button.

Button "Default"

When this button is pressed, all parameters are moved to the standard position.

Why the application does not work?

1. You use the Internet Explorer.

2. Your version is very outdated and does not support some functions (Please try updating your browser).
